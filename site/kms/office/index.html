Office Activation¶

Usage¶

Start a Command Prompt as an Administrator.

cd C:\Program Files\Microsoft Office\Office15
cscript ospp.vbs /inpkey:<GVLK>
cscript ospp.vbs /sethst:kms.s10e.net
cscript ospp.vbs /act

Note

You are supposed to replace <GVLK> with your desired edition and product GVLK Key.
